#### Controls

- **WASD** or **Arrow Keys**: Classic controller movement. Up is drop.
- **Q** & **E** or **/** & **Shift**: Rotate.
- **Space**, **F**, or **R**: Reflect.
- **Tab**, **CapsLock**, **Z**, or **X**: Hold for later.
